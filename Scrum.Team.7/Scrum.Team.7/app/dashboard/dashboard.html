What is Survometer?

We've all particapted in online surveys from various parts of the NICS but where is our data going?

NICS have identified a risk in the continued use of 3rd party survey tools, most of which store what is often very senstive information outside the UK.

Survometer is the NICS Survey tool. It is:

  • A free to use premium tool for use by all Civil Servants.
  • Owned and maintained by the NICS.
  • Can be extended for use in public facing surveys.
